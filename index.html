Privacy Policy Overview

This privacy policy governs the use of all mobile applications developed by HakomSoft (XAEX APPS) ("Abdelhakim Elmoumen"). Our applications are ad-supported and may integrate third-party services for advertising, analytics, and functionality enhancement.

Data Collection and Use

We collect user data to improve app functionality and enhance the user experience. The types of data we collect include:

1. Personal Information:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address, Android ID)
  • IP address and device model
  • App usage data (e.g., session duration, features used)

2. Purpose of Data Collection:

  • Provide essential app functionality
  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Analyze, optimize, and improve app performance
  • Prevent fraud and ensure app security

3. Data Processing:

  • Data may be processed by third-party services according to their privacy policies.
  • These third parties include Huawei Petal Ads.

User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access Your Data: You may request a copy of the data we collect.
  • Request Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising: You can disable personalized ads through your device settings or Huawei privacy options.
  • Restrict Data Processing: You can limit how your data is processed by contacting us.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our applications may use cookies, pixel tracking, and similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can disable tracking via your device settings.

Data Security

We implement standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure.
